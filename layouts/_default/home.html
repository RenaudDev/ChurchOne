Our Values

SCRIPTURES

We value the supreme authority of scripture.

PARISH-CENTRED MINISTRY

We value equipping parishes to be the focus for ministry and evangelism.

RELATIONSHIPS AND TRANSPARENCY

We value conducting the business and ministry of the diocese based on healthy Christ-like relationships and transparency.

UNITY

We value walking together with love and respect even when differing on non-essential matters.

ANGLICAN IDENTITY

We value nurturing liturgical and sacramental worship in the Anglican way as expressed through the catholic, evangelical, and charismatic streams.

GOVERNANCE

We value being led by shepherd bishops who serve our parishes, province, and the worldwide communion.
